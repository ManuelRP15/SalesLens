Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Salesforce Translation Toolkit (the “Extension”) handles information when you use the browser extension.

The Extension is developed and published by Manuel Roldán.

1. Purpose of the Extension

Salesforce Translation Toolkit is a browser extension designed to help Salesforce developers and administrators inspect, search, and edit Salesforce translations directly within their Salesforce environment.

The Extension currently supports Salesforce Custom Labels, Custom Fields, and Custom Picklist Values.

2. Information the Extension Accesses

To provide its core functionality, the Extension may access information available within the Salesforce organization that the user is actively working with. This may include:

* Salesforce metadata related to supported translation types.
* Translation values and language information.
* Metadata identifiers and API names required to identify Salesforce elements.
* Information necessary to display, edit, and update supported translations.
* Information related to the user’s Workspace, such as tracked metadata elements and translation changes.

The Extension only accesses Salesforce information necessary to provide its functionality.

3. Storage of Information

The Extension uses the browser’s local extension storage to save information such as:

* User preferences and extension settings.
* Selected languages and configuration.
* Workspace items and tracked changes.
* Workspace history and related metadata.
* Other information required to maintain the user’s workflow between sessions.

This information is stored locally in the user’s browser through the browser extension storage APIs.

The Extension does not operate a separate cloud database for storing users’ Workspace data.

4. Salesforce Authentication and Data

The Extension is designed to work with Salesforce organizations where the user is already authenticated.

The Extension may communicate with the Salesforce organization the user is actively using in order to retrieve or update supported translation information.

The Extension does not ask users to provide their Salesforce password to the Extension.

The Extension does not sell, rent, or otherwise monetize Salesforce data accessed through the Extension.

5. Data Sharing

The Extension does not sell, rent, or share users’ personal information or Salesforce data with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes.

The Extension does not use Salesforce data for targeted advertising.

The Extension does not maintain a separate service that collects or stores users’ Salesforce translation data for unrelated purposes.

6. Data Collection

The Extension does not intentionally collect personal information for advertising, profiling, or tracking purposes.

The Extension does not use users’ Salesforce data to build advertising profiles.

Any information accessed by the Extension is used solely to provide the functionality described in the Chrome Web Store listing.

7. Data Security

The Extension is designed to minimize the amount of data it stores and processes.

Workspace information and extension preferences are stored using browser extension storage mechanisms. Salesforce data is accessed only when required to provide the Extension’s functionality.

Users should be aware that no software or data transmission system can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

8. Data Retention and Deletion

Information stored locally by the Extension remains available in the user’s browser until it is removed by the user, cleared through the Extension’s functionality, or removed through the browser’s extension data management mechanisms.

Users can delete Workspace information using the Workspace controls provided by the Extension.

Uninstalling the Extension may remove locally stored extension data according to the browser’s extension data management behavior.

9. Third-Party Services

The Extension interacts with Salesforce services as required to provide its functionality.

Salesforce is a separate service governed by its own privacy policies and terms. The Extension does not control how Salesforce processes information within the Salesforce platform.

The Extension is not affiliated with, sponsored by, or endorsed by Salesforce, Inc.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time to reflect changes to the Extension or its functionality.

When significant changes are made, the updated version will be published at the same location with a new “Last updated” date.

11. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or the Extension’s privacy practices, you can contact the publisher through the contact information provided on the Extension’s store listing or its associated project repository.
